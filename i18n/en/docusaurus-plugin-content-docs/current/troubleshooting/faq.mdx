# FAQ

## When does a question belong in the FAQ?

If the same question comes up repeatedly and can usually be answered in a few sentences, it is a good candidate for the FAQ.

## When should it become a standalone page?

If the answer needs detailed steps, sample configurations, risk notes, or multiple scenario branches, it should become its own document.

## How should the FAQ be organized?

Grouping by phases such as "before setup", "during setup", "policy and security", and "during troubleshooting" keeps navigation simple.

## What is the free quota best suited for?

It is best for a first validation run with a small number of engineering or operations devices, where the goal is to confirm policy, relay, and audit behavior.

## When should a team evaluate private or hybrid deployment?

As soon as compliance, logging boundaries, traffic control, or integration requirements become important enough that hosted defaults are no longer sufficient.
