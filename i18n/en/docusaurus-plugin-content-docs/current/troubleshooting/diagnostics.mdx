# Diagnostics

Troubleshooting works better when it follows a repeatable flow. That also makes the process easier to reuse in blog posts and showcase stories.

## Step 1: confirm scope

- Is the issue limited to one device or affecting the whole workspace?
- Is it isolated to one region, account, or time window, or broadly reproducible?

## Step 2: collect signals

- Current workspace, policy template, and relay mode
- Device online state, session analytics, and latency indicators
- Request timestamp, request origin, error logs, and security events

## Step 3: trace the path

Move from identity validation to access policy, relay path, and then the destination device. Avoid changing multiple variables at once.

## Step 4: document the conclusion

Capture the root cause, temporary mitigation, long-term fix, and whether policy templates, monitoring, or audit retention rules need to be updated.
