# Diagnostics

Troubleshooting works better when it follows a repeatable flow. That also makes the process easier to reuse in blog posts and showcase stories.

## Step 1: confirm scope

- Is the issue limited to one service or affecting the whole project?
- Is it isolated to one region, account, or time window, or broadly reproducible?

## Step 2: collect signals

- Current published configuration version
- Upstream address and health check result
- Request timestamp, request origin, and error logs

## Step 3: trace the path

Move from the entry point layer to the forwarding layer and then to the upstream service. Avoid changing multiple variables at once.

## Step 4: document the conclusion

Capture the root cause, temporary mitigation, long-term fix, and whether new documentation or monitoring rules are needed.
