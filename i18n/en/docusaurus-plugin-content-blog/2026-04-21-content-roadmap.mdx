To make the help center valuable quickly, it helps to fill in the following topics first:

{/* truncate */}

1. Formal explanations for Headscale, zero-trust networking, relay modes, and identity controls.
2. High-priority troubleshooting content for offline devices, denied policies, latency, and audit anomalies.
3. Scenario guides for distributed engineering, IT operations and MSP teams, and enterprise remote work.

If the product has a regular release cadence, the blog should also carry release notes, pricing or packaging changes, and migration guidance so the history stays easy to follow.
